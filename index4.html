FAQ

Common Questions

What AFS ranges do you supply?
+
We supply a complete range of AFS grades from fine to coarse specifications (AFS 40-70), customized to meet your specific foundry requirements. Our technical team provides consultation to select the optimal grade for your casting process.
Do you offer customized sand grades?
+
Yes, we specialize in creating custom sand grades based on AFS specifications. Our advanced classification system allows us to tailor grain size distribution, moisture content, and purity levels to your exact requirements.
What are your supply options?
+
We offer both bulk industrial supply for large-scale operations and bagged options for smaller requirements. Contract-based supply agreements are available for consistent, long-term partnerships with priority scheduling.
How do you ensure quality consistency?
+
Every batch undergoes rigorous lab testing including sieve analysis, moisture testing, and clay content verification. We maintain strict quality control protocols throughout production and provide detailed batch-wise test reports.
What are typical delivery timelines?
+
Standard orders are fulfilled within 5-7 business days. Custom specifications may require 10-14 days. Contract customers receive priority scheduling and expedited delivery options.
