Technology & Methodology

Controlled, step-wise processing for repeatable sand performance.

  1. 1

    Raw Sand Sourcing – High-quality mines

    Carefully selected deposits with consistent silica content and low inherent impurities.

  2. 2

    Washing & Scrubbing – Clay & impurity removal

    Multi-stage washing and intensive scrubbing to break coatings and wash away fines.

  3. 3

    Fluidizer Separation – Dust & micro-impurities

    Fluidizer systems separate light particles and ultra-fines to achieve cleaner sand.

  4. 4

    Classification – Accurate AFS grading

    Controlled classification lines generate stable AFS bands aligned with foundry requirements.

  5. 5

    Dewatering – Moisture control

    Dewatering equipment and controlled drying ensure moisture is within tight specification windows.

  6. 6

    Drying & Testing – Lab verification

    Lab checks for sieve distribution, clay content, LOI, and moisture to confirm batch approval.

  7. 7

    Packaging & Dispatch – Bulk or bagged

    Final product is dispatched in bulk or bags with clear batch identification for traceability.

FAQs

Answers to common questions from foundry and procurement teams.

What AFS ranges do you supply?

A wide range of AFS grades typically from 35 to 70 can be supplied, with specific bands aligned to your molding and core processes.

Do you offer customized sand grades?

Yes, custom AFS distributions and blends are developed based on your casting requirements, existing recipes, and trial results.

What are your bulk vs bag supply options?

Sand is supplied in bulk tankers, loose trucks, or bagged formats depending on your plant layout and handling systems.

How frequently is quality tested?

Each batch undergoes lab checks for key parameters, with additional in-process sampling to maintain consistency across shipments.

What are typical delivery timelines?

Delivery timelines are planned against your call-offs and contracts, with buffer capacity to handle surge requirements where possible.
