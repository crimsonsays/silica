Technology & Methodology

Our Production Process

Advanced multi-stage washing and classification system ensuring consistent quality and purity.

1

Raw Sand Sourcing

High-quality mines with consistent silica content and minimal impurities are carefully selected for raw material extraction.

2

Washing & Scrubbing

Multi-stage washing process removes clay, silt, and surface impurities through intensive scrubbing and water treatment.

3

Fluidizer Separation

Advanced fluidizer technology eliminates dust and micro-impurities, ensuring ultra-clean sand particles.

4

Classification – Accurate AFS Grading

Precision classification systems ensure accurate AFS grading according to customer specifications and industry standards.

5

Dewatering – Moisture Control

State-of-the-art dewatering equipment maintains optimal moisture levels for consistent casting performance.

6

Drying & Testing – Lab Verification

Comprehensive laboratory testing including sieve analysis, moisture testing, and chemical composition verification.

7

Packaging & Dispatch

Final product packaged in bulk or bags with proper labeling and dispatched through efficient logistics network.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

What AFS ranges do you supply?

We supply a complete range of AFS grades from 35 to 70, covering fine, medium, and coarse sand classifications suitable for various foundry applications including molding and core-making operations.

Do you offer customized sand grades?

Yes, we specialize in custom AFS-based grading tailored to your specific casting requirements. Our technical team works closely with clients to develop sand specifications that optimize their manufacturing processes.

What are your bulk vs bag supply options?

We offer flexible supply options including bulk truckload deliveries for high-volume operations and bagged supplies (25kg/50kg bags) for smaller requirements. Custom packaging solutions are also available based on your needs.

How frequently is quality tested?

Every production batch undergoes comprehensive laboratory testing including sieve analysis, moisture content, clay impurity levels, and AFS grading verification. Test certificates are provided with each shipment.

What are typical delivery timelines?

Standard delivery timelines range from 3-7 days depending on location and order volume. For contract-based supplies, we maintain buffer inventory to ensure just-in-time delivery aligned with your production schedules.
