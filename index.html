FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What AFS ranges do you supply? +
We supply a complete range of AFS grades from fine to coarse specifications, customized to meet your specific foundry requirements. Our technical team can help you select the optimal grade for your casting process.
Do you offer customized sand grades? +
Yes, we specialize in creating custom sand grades based on AFS specifications. Our advanced classification system allows us to tailor grain size distribution, moisture content, and purity levels to your exact needs.
What are your supply options? +
We offer both bulk industrial supply for large-scale operations and bagged options for smaller requirements. We also provide contract-based supply agreements for consistent, long-term partnerships.
How do you ensure quality consistency? +
Every batch undergoes rigorous lab testing including sieve analysis, moisture testing, and clay content verification. We maintain strict quality control protocols throughout the production process and provide batch-wise test reports.
What are your typical delivery timelines? +
We maintain efficient supply chains to ensure on-time delivery. Standard orders are typically fulfilled within 5-7 business days, while custom specifications may require 10-14 days. Contract customers receive priority scheduling.
